The data for table 1 can be found in "processed_data/ueg_cbs/*gamma.csv" files.
For example, the CCSD data is in "processed_data/ueg_cbs/ccsd_corr_cbs_gamma.csv" at rs = 2.
To get the triples (CCSDT, DCSDT, ring-CCSDT) data, go to "processed_data/ueg_cbs/diff_{method}_dcsd_corr_cbs_gamma.csv"
and add the appropriate energy to the CBS DCSD energy given in "processed_data/ueg_cbs/dcsd_corr_cbs_gamma.csv".
